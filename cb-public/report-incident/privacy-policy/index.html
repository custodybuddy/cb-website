CustodyBuddy ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the information you share through our web application and related services (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information. Name, email, and optional case identifiers that you provide.
  • Incident Data. Dates, narratives, parties/children involved, and evidence you upload—used only to generate your report.
  • Technical Data. Minimal, anonymized events (e.g., step views, export clicks) to improve reliability; no content is logged.
  • Local Storage. Draft data may be stored in your browser to prevent loss; this does not leave your device unless you export or submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Generate AI-assisted summaries and structured, court-ready exports you control.
  • Provide accessibility, security, and reliability improvements.
  • Comply with applicable laws and enforce our Terms of Service.

Lawful basis (Canada/PIPEDA): your consent and reasonable purposes related to the service you request.

3. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed to provide the App. Demo-mode data is not stored. Evidence files are removed after export unless you choose to retain them.

4. Data Security

  • HTTPS in transit; access controls at rest.
  • Upload scanning, MIME sniffing, and type/size allow‑lists.
  • Principle of least privilege for staff and systems.

No system is perfectly secure; we continuously monitor and mitigate risk.

5. AI Processing

We use trusted AI providers to transform your inputs into summaries. We do not use your private submissions to train public models. Inputs/outputs are encrypted in transit and discarded after your session per provider terms.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, download, and correct your information.
  • Request deletion of personal data we store.
  • Withdraw consent for processing not required to deliver the service.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use third‑party tracking cookies. We use localStorage to autosave drafts and preserve progress. You can clear local data via your browser or the App’s “Reset Form.”

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is for adult users involved in family law or co‑parenting. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children under 16.

9. Data Disclosure

We never sell or rent your data. We may disclose information only to comply with lawful requests (e.g., court orders) or protect users’ rights, property, or safety.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy. The effective date above reflects the latest version. Material changes will be announced in‑app and/or by email when appropriate.
